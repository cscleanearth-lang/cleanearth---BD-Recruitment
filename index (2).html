Senior Manager, Business Development & Strategic Growth

WaterEnvironment & SustainabilityClean Energy

Cleanearth seeks an ambitious and commercially driven Senior Manager to accelerate growth across its Water, Environment & Sustainability, and Clean Energy businesses — leading client acquisition, key account growth, and market expansion across Ghana's industrial sector.

Position Summary

This role will lead new client acquisition, strategic relationship development, key account growth, and market expansion initiatives. The successful candidate will be responsible for identifying opportunities, influencing senior decision-makers, building strategic partnerships, and converting opportunities into long-term commercial relationships. This is a high-impact role suited to a commercially minded professional who thrives in entrepreneurial environments and is excited by the opportunity to help build one of Africa's leading environmental solutions platforms.

Key Responsibilities

Business Development

  • Identify and pursue new opportunities across industrial sectors.
  • Develop and maintain a robust sales pipeline.
  • Build relationships with senior executives and key decision-makers.
  • Lead opportunity qualification and conversion.
  • Drive proposal development and bid management.

Strategic Accounts

  • Manage key customer relationships.
  • Expand revenue within existing accounts.
  • Identify cross-selling opportunities across service lines.

Market Development

  • Monitor market trends and emerging opportunities.
  • Identify new sectors, geographies and service opportunities.
  • Support introduction of new products and services.

Partnerships

  • Develop partnerships with industry associations.
  • Build relationships with technology partners.
  • Support engagement with development partners and financiers.

Commercial Planning

  • Maintain CRM discipline.
  • Support forecasting and revenue planning.
  • Report pipeline performance to leadership.

Candidate Requirements

Experience

  • 8–12 years of progressive experience.
  • At least 5 years in business development, commercial management or strategic sales.
  • Demonstrated success selling solutions into industrial markets.
